Step-by-Step Procedures

For Users:

  1. Download & Sign Up:
    • Verify your phone number and email.
    • Upload a profile picture and at least two additional images (maximum of five).
    • Select interests and hobbies for better matchmaking.
  2. Complete Your Profile:
    • Add optional details like religion, net worth, education, and preferences to enhance matching accuracy.
  3. Join Events:
    • Navigate to the “Join Events” page to find and join public or private events.
    • Use QR codes or links to access private hubs.

For Event Organizers:

  1. Create Events:
    • Go to the “My Events” tab and select “Create Event.”
    • Fill in event details, upload images, and select the location, date, and time.
    • Choose whether the event is public or private.
  2. Set Privacy Levels:
    • For public events, attendees can find them in the “Join Events” tab.
    • For private events, only invited users with the link or QR code can join.
  3. Share Access Codes:
    • Distribute access codes manually for added security.
    • Ensure attendees are within the venue perimeter for entry.

For Businesses:

  1. Create Business Hubs:
    • Select “Business/Workplace” during event creation.
    • Add details, upload images, and input payout information.
  2. Renew Weekly:
    • Business hubs expire every 7 days to maintain current user engagement.
  3. Share QR Codes:
    • Provide access codes and QR codes to customers for secure networking.

For Travelers:

  1. Access Flight Mingle:
    • Tap “Flight Mingle” on the home page and add flight details manually or by scanning a boarding pass.
  2. Network at the Airport:
    • Join the hub to connect with passengers, crew, or pilots.

Partnership Agreement

Please review the terms of our partnership carefully. This Agreement is between Ingle Meet ("Company") and you, the Influencer.

1. Purpose

You agree to promote Ingle Meet and its features to your audience, creating or sharing promotional content according to our guidelines.

2. Deliverables

You agree to:

  • Create or share pictures/videos promoting Ingle Meet.
  • Tag Ingle Meet and use the #IngleMeet hashtag.
  • Optionally promote upcoming Ingle-powered events in your city.

3. Compensation

Earnings are based on:

  • Promotion Commissions: 2% flat rate on all event creations in your state for 6 months (max $50,000).
  • Partnership Influencing: 5% commission on all connections made via companies (hotels, venues, etc.) you help partner with Ingle Meet, for 6 months (max $50,000).
  • Personal Event Hosting Bonus: Additional 5% on your initial commission for hosting your own Ingle Meet event.
  • Optional Bonuses: Potential 0.5% extra for high-performing content or viral campaigns for events during that week.

Note: Compensation is tied to events hosted in your state/region. You can filter events by city within the Ingle Meet app to estimate potential earnings.

4. Content Rights

You grant Ingle Meet permission to repost, share, or use submitted promotional content in our marketing efforts, with attribution to you.

5. Brand Guidelines

  • Content must not be offensive, discriminatory, or misleading.
  • Content must align with Ingle Meet's tone: bold, classy, clever, connection-driven.
  • You must strictly adhere to the rules and regulations of your country and the social media platforms used.
  • Ingle Meet reserves the final say on content alignment and commission eligibility.

6. Term & Termination

This agreement is valid for 6 months upon signing, unless terminated by either party with 7 days' written notice. Breaching terms may result in immediate termination and forfeiture of commissions.

7. Confidentiality

Proprietary details like performance insights or specific rates must remain confidential.
